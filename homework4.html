2. Create Cable Tie Mount

Step 1: Create New Design

Click [New] → [Mixed Design] → [New]

Create new design for cable tie mount

Step 2: Input AI Prompt in Autodesk Assistant

Strictly create a single solid finished model according to the following parameters. No free deformation, no solid block blanks, no extra protruding structures.

Overall dimensions: base length 45mm, width 20mm, thickness 4mm, all corners R1 fillet.

Base is a horizontal rectangular flat plate. At the center position of both left and right ends, drill a through circular mounting hole with diameter 4mm.

In the exact center of the base, two symmetrical vertical plates extend vertically upward. Plate height 15mm, thickness 4mm, inner spacing between the two plates is 8mm, forming a front-to-back through rectangular slot for threading nylon cable ties to secure wire bundles.

Plates and base are integrally formed, structurally complete, ready for direct use after molding without secondary modification, compliant with 3D printing standards.
